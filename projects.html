UX Portfolio

Inframent - Mobile and WebApp using Material 3

Inframent was a construction technology startup I co-founded. I was responsible for all aspects of product development, starting with rapid prototyping to validate the idea to fully fledged designs for both a mobile application and a web application. Checke out the video where I show you each stage of my process and the end results as well!

Focus - onboarding flow

Working on Focus as a UX/UI designer was very interesting because it is a SaaS solution that employs product led growth strategies, something I'm really interested in. Check out how I did the onboarding flow and what was my rationale behind my decisions.

Livery - pricing page

A small mockup of a pricing page for a complicated, live interactive video infrastructure product called Livery. I showcase my mockup and the reasoning behind the choices I made and how it stacks up to the final result the company went with later.

Bicycle AI Glide - AI customer service Chrome Extension

At Bicycle AI I was responsible for the conversational UX of our product, a Chrome Extension that would integrate with Intercom to help customer service agents reply to messages quicker and more accurately. With Bicycle AI I had the opportunity to attend Y Combinator in 2017 as well. Watch the video to discover the challenges I had to tackle while working on a highly innovative product like this. Mind you, this was way before ChatGPT came onto the scene. Nowadays this kind of solution would be much easier, but back then, the UX was truly pioneering.
